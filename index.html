You have things to do. Searching endlessly for the government information you need doesn’t have to be one of them.

Passports. Social Security benefits. Tax forms. Small business help. There are so many reasons to need government information and services, but finding them can be tough. Let us help. USA Gov is the federal program that guides you to tips and tools in English and in Spanish from hundreds of government agencies, departments, and programs. Online, by phone or chat, and in print—we help make everyday life a little easier.

What can I find online from USA Gov?

Explore USA.gov for government info on benefits, health, jobs, and more. And discover fun educational tools at Kids.gov.

Can I speak with someone by phone or online chat?

Call 1-844-USA-GOV1 or go to USA.gov/chat to connect with specialists who can answer your questions.

How can I get information in print?

Visit USA.gov/handbook. The free Consumer Action Handbook helps you solve complaints, avoid ID theft, and shop smarter.

Español

Where can I find information in Spanish?

Visit GobiernoUSA.gov for services and information in Spanish on immigration, housing, financial assistance, and more.
